Corporate Broadcasts (Corporate TV or Broadcasting)

It has been exactly 19 years.

In those years, -when the Internet was not so common and fast- we were delivering the one-hour content we prepared every week to Digiturk with old-style tapes.

These broadcasts made via satellite, delivered the voice of the company’s headquarters Sütlüce to all Beko and Arçelik Authorized Dealers. Over time, next to Arçelik and Beko, we could not put the word “customer” , we have made many new friends and companions.

As the time went by, the internet has become faster, widespread and has become a corporate broadcasting infrastructure.

The development of technologies in this field; helps us deliver more diverse, accurate content to ‘target audiences’ faster and with much higher quality images.

Compiled from the world’s most prestigious news and content providers; The broadcasts we produce by blending corporate news with up to date content that changes every day, reach over 30 thousand employees of different institutions.

Sharing a content with them in the office corridors, in the cafeteria, sometimes while waiting for the elevator, which they may not have seen yet but we know they will be interested in...

To deliver live calls of CEOs to the employees of a regional directorate in very distant geographies...

To inform them of the social responsibility projects of the institutions they work with, the success they have won, the awards they have received, and to share their pride...

To bring an important corporate announcement that disappears among the e-mail traffic to the screens by visually enriching it and increasing its impact.

Moreover, to deliver all these with brand new content every new day, with the love and excitement of the first day.

All these must have been the basis of our long-lasting friendship with the industry’s largest organizations.
