Production

For the SG P, there is no distinction between large and small production. Every work, regardless of the budgetary size of the production, is carried out in accordance with the essence and requirements of that work, first considering the “quality criterion”. The important thing is that the work prepared and the result obtained is impressive and extraordinary at the maximum level.

One of the criteria that has made SG P indispensable in the eyes of its customers for 19 years is the “organizational ability” and thus the “speed and agility” reflexes it has gained. In every process of the production, the right team and equipment are brought together with the right planning and organization, and they pass the next stage and every work is finalized with great care and meticulousness.

The “core team” that has been working within SG P for years never changes, but grows bigger or smaller according to the needs of the production. Whether it’s a giant commercial production that requires a 100-person set crew, or a studio shot with a crew of 3; For SG P, they are all productions with the same pursuit of quality and the same meticulous work. Again, from this point of view, SG P cooperates with world-renowned directors and technical teams, each of whom are experts in their fields, in addition to its experienced directors.

In fact, the speed and budget advantages that SG P provides to all its customers is the outcome of this perspective.
